01 — Cash Flow

Find Your Money Leaks

You don't lose money all at once. You lost it slowly. Small charges compound. Quick swipes, subscriptions, and delivery apps separate you from the total.

🔒
Fixed Expenses
Rent, phone bill, predictable costs
🌊
Variable Expenses
Food, transport, shopping — leaks live here
🎯
The 50/30/20 Rule
Needs / Wants / Savings

Budgeting isn't a punishment — it's visibility. When you can see your spending, patterns become obvious. And once you find the leaks, you decide what stays.

04 — Investing

Your Biggest Advantage Is Time

Your biggest advantage right now isn't money. It's time. Money earns returns. Then those returns earn returns. That's compound growth.

ETFs & mutual funds — spread risk across many companies
Start small — even a few dollars at a time counts
Don't day trade — consistency beats timing every time

06 — Student Loans

Clarity Removes Fear

You don't need a full repayment strategy today. You just need to understand what you signed up for. Once you do — it becomes manageable.

Know your loan type — subsidized loans don't grow while you're in school, while unsubsidized loans do
Interest compounds quietly — even small amounts added over time can significantly increase your total repayment
Uncertainty creates stress — clarity creates control

First steps to take

1

Log in to your loan servicer and identify your loan type

2

Check your current balance and interest rate

3

Understand when repayment starts and what you'll owe

4

Explore refinancing options to potentially lower your rate
